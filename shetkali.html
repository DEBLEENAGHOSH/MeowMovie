Shwetkali (2023)


About the Series


    Shwetkali is Bengali Thriller Drama web-series of 1 season consisting 8 episodes. It was created and directed by Sani Ghosh Ray. The show marks Oindrilla Sen's debut on a digital platform.The series is premiered on ZEE5 on 24 February 2023.
