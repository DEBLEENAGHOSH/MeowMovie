Chengiz (2023)


About the movie


    Chengiz is an 2023 Indian Bengali-language period action thriller film co-written and directed by Rajesh Ganguly from a story written by Neeraj Pandey and himself. It is produced by Jeet, Gopal Madnani and Amit Jumrani.The music was composed by Kaushik Guddu and Aneek Dhar, while the action sequences were designed by Stunt Silva.

Cast


  • Jeet in dual role as- Jaidev Singh aka Chengiz, a dreaded gangster of Calcutta and Daniel D'Souza, a software engineer.
  • Rohit Roy as ACP Samir Sinha
  • Susmita Chatterjee as Nandini Sen, Chengiz's girlfriend-cum-wife
  • Rooqma Ray as Debjani, Debu and Rupali's daughter
  • Shataf Figar as Omar Khalid alias Nalli Bhai, Central Kolkata gangster
  • Indrajit Mazumder as Mughal Abbas
  • Sudip Mukherjee as Commissioner Amit Dhar
  • Biswarup Biswas as Kallu, an assassin
  • Annine Sen as Pubali, Inspahani's wife
  • Ayesha Bhattacharya as Sonali, Andrew's wife
  • Mousumi Das as Rupali, Debu's wife
  • Avrajit Chakraborty as Ispahani
  • Sam Mukherjee as Andrew


Where to watch


    Chengiz was theatrically released in India on 21 April 2023 in Bengali, along with its dubbed version in Hindi. It is the first Bengali film to be released simultaneously in Bengali and Hindi languages.Although officially nothing has been revealed that in which OTT platform it is going to be released on Amazon Prime Video as well as on Hoichoi.
