Indubala Bhaater Hotel (2023)


About the Series


    Indubala Bhaater Hotel is a 2023 drama Bengali web series available on the streaming platform Hoichoi. The series, based on the eponymous Bengali novel by Kallor Lahiri, is about the life and struggles of Indubala, the protagonist woman.It released on Hoichoi app on 8th March,2023.

Cast


  • Subhashree Ganguly as Indubala Mallick
  • Sneha Chatterjee as Lachhmi
  • Debapratim Dasgupta as Dhananjay alias Dhana
  • Parijat Chaudhuri as young Indubala
  • Angana Roy as Sanchari
  • Rahul Banerjee as Pradip, Indubala's younger son
  • Pratik Dutta as Ratanlal Mallick, Indubala's husband


Where to watch


    Currently you are able to watch "Indubala Bhaater Hotel" streaming on Hoichoi, Amazon Prime.
